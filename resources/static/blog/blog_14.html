Top Resources and Platforms for Finding Student Accommodation in London

December 16, 2024 | 5 MINS
Universities Housing Services University Run Accomodation Portals University Accommodation Offices Housing Websites Special Offers and Discounts Guranteed Accomodation Accomodation Platform for Students UniAcoo Student.com SpareRoom Rightmove Zoopla Conclusion

As one of the largest, bustling, and multicultural cities, London has a lot to offer students. However, in today’s competitive and fast-renting environment of London, looking for affordable and appropriate student accommodation can be a boring task. With the increase in rents and a huge number of students in London, searching for the most appropriate accommodation can be rather a problem. Nevertheless, there are lots of means and avenues available that can help you locate a suitable student accommodation in Sheffield Uk that meets your expectations and is within your financial capacity.

This complex task obliges us to separate and analyze the major, in our opinion, resources and platforms where one can look for student accommodation in London: websites, universities’ provided services, and social networks specifically oriented to the students.

Universities Housing Services

University Run Accomodation Portals

In most cases, universities in London also have accommodation services or portals to provide even greater ease of finding places to live on or off the campus. This includes listings and advice about housing specifically for students and first-year students may sometimes be assured of accommodation as well. Follow these tips on how to best use the accommodation services available at your university:

University Accommodation Offices

Just like the numerous institutions of higher education in London such as University College London (UCL), King’s College London or Imperial College London, all have housing offices where students can check for on-campus housing, privately rented accommodation or student flats located near the campus.

Housing Websites

It is common for many universities to work with providers of student accommodation and have portals where students can search for available housing options. Similarly, these portals are usually informative regarding costs, locations, and how to apply for housing.

#Special Offers and Discounts

Discounts for students’ accommodation may also be available from universities which partner with local landlords or have a base close to the university, suggesting affordable housing or help in locating suitable options.

Guranteed-Accomodation

If your undergraduate studies commence and this is your first year, it is likely that your institution will offer you accommodation managed by them which is considered quite safe and convenient. This is invaluable especially for new Asian students or those new to London.

Be sure to consult your university’s website for further details on the available types of housing and application timelines.

Accommodation Platforms for Students

There are many online sites that specifically assist students in need of cheap accommodation. Such sites often advertise privately let student accommodation, lodgers or substitute housing units for rent in the market and such availability hails a variety of prices and styles for preference to students. Among the most widely used platforms are:

UniAcoo

UniAcco is another platform appealing to students seeking accommodation in various cities around the UK, notably London. It enables students to look for student housing that meets their financial capability, the areas they would like to stay in and the type of housing. UniAcco works with several landlords and private rental agents who provide a range of accommodations from shared student housing to self-contained one bed flats.

Key Features
● Large numbers of student housing options including purpose-built student accommodations (PBSA)
● Location, price, and amenities search filters

Booking and contract assistance

student.com

Student.com is one of the largest websites that facilitate the owners and landlords of student accommodation in London and other parts of the world. The site collaborates with multiple landlords to present many affordable housing alternatives. In case if one has several choices, one may narrow down his or her choice by filtering the price range, distance from the university, and the type of the accommodation.

Key Features:
● Numerous types of accommodation including halls of residence, studios, or shared flats
● Advanced search filters, for example, transport connections to the area or local services see more in specific tabs.
● 24/7 assistance in endangered booking processes.

SpareRoom

SpareRoom is a good choice for students searching for a room in a shared flat or house. The website has many useful pages with many listings and options of specific searches like per location, prices, or photos and comments of prior residents. If you are willing to share your home with others for finding a room and searching in busy areas of London, then SpareRoom is a good platform for you.

Key Features:
● A long list of available rooms for rent in shared accommodations
● Ability to ‘Buddy Up’ with other renters, if desired, and look for people to share the accommodation with
● Listings that are constantly added which help in getting the new alternatives

Rightmove

Rightmove is particularly popular when it comes to market research for rental properties, especially such properties as student accommodation. Although Rightmove is not limited to the students only site, it does have many listings which are quite student-oriented within the London area. You can sort the search results according to the amount of rent, geographical location, and the amount of bedrooms offered

Key Features:
● Filters that allow flatting including different types of property types such as flats, houses, shared rooms
● Different types of A dedicated filter for price, area, and operational features
● This allows properties to be contrasted with one another, as well as providing a place for persistence in saving properties.

Zoopla

Zoopla is another leading property platform in the UK which provides available rented residential properties which include student accommodation in London as well as other rental properties. Further, it’s possible to search by the location, budget, and amenities along with letting which are usually represented by private landlords permitting better rental conditions.

Key Features:
● Variety of rental properties and apartments
● Search for property with specific filters
● Price range tracker to notify you in case of any changes

Conclusion

When it comes to student accommodation in London, the process may look complicated; however, this is false. A student can, for instance, search for university-run accommodation, a flat share, or even a private residence, with the assistance of sites such as UniAcco, Student.com, SpareRoom and Rightmove. There are also social media groups, university housing departments, and rental agencies that focus on the problem who seem to be of help. If a student takes proper advantage of these services and starts looking early enough, he or she should have no problems finding accommodation in London.
